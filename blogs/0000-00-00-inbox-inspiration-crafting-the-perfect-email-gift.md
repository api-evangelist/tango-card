A guide to sending email gifts (a digital present delivered to a recipient's inbox as a redeemable code, personalized message, and claim link) that feel personal and get redeemed. It covers five steps including personalization and mobile-first design, noting personalized subject lines are 26% more likely to be opened and over half of emails open on mobile.
